Nanotechnology

What is Nanotechnology?

It is the manipulation of matter on a near-atomic scale to produce new structures, materials and devices. The technology promises scientific advancement in many sectors such as medicine, consumer products, energy, materials and manufacturing.



Nanotechnology

Is a field of science and engineering that deals with the study and manipulation of matter on an atomic, molecular, and supramolecular scale. It involves the design, production, and application of materials, devices, and systems with unique properties and functions at the nanoscale.
The development of nanotechnology has opened up new possibilities in various fields such as medicine, electronics, energy, and materials science. Researchers are exploring ways to use nanoparticles for drug delivery, cancer treatment, solar cells, and more.
